My Experience in Tax

Tax Intern:

I conducted tax for non-profit organization, individual income, and nonresident alien.

Company: GRF CPAs & Advisors.

Company: Gannon Small Business Development Center.

As an international student, my first experience with tax was challenging. The first time I did my taxes, I filled out as a resident, rather than using the 1040-NR for non-residents. Despite being an accounting student, my freshman knowledge was limited, and I had to research extensively to file the 1040-X. While I eventually resolved it thanks to my background in accounting, I noticed so many international students faced similar issues annually. With little support from the university, I started helping students with their tax forms, guided by a CPA and my professors.

Tax Intern

At my time at GRF CPAs & Advisors as an audit intern, I also got the opportunity to worked on tax returns specifically for nonprofit organizations. I assisted in filling necessary tax forms focusing on Form 990, Form 990-EZ, and Form 990-PF.

Tax Volunteer

I assisted local residents in Erie in filing their income tax along with GSBD's assistance program. It mostly helps elderly people or those who face challenges in filing their taxes themselves.

Duration: June - August 2024
