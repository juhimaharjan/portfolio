My Experience in Audit

Audit Intern:

I individually performed audits for corporate, international, and non-profit clients. Local non-profit clients mostly based on Washington DC.

Company: GRF CPAs & Advisors.

Audit Testing

I independently conducted audits for various financial areas, including payroll, credit cards, accounts payable, cash receipts and disbursements, prepaid, and accruals. This work was performed for corporate, international, and non-profit clients, ensuring accurate financial reporting.

Risk Assessment

I documented key risks during walkthroughs and field testing, identifying potential discrepancies. Using tools like CaseWare and Excel, I performed materiality testing to support accurate and reliable audit conclusions.

Internal Controls and Compliance

I conducted internal control tests to assess compliance with GAAP standards, resolving discrepancies and ensuring clients adhered to established accounting principles.

One of the clients I worked closely with during my audit internship was the Jane Goodall Foundation, renowned for their long-term research on chimpanzees in Gombe Stream National Park, Tanzania. I had the opportunity to perform audit testing, conduct risk assessments, and assist with forming audit opinions for the foundation. At the conclusion of my internship, I was able to present a comprehensive audit report on the foundation and its financials. In addition to the Jane Goodall Foundation, I also contributed to the audit reports of several other organizations, expanding my experience across various industries.

Software used: Caseware and Microsoft Excel

Duration: June - August 2024
