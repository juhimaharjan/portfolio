Euphoric Clay & Pots

Inventory & Sales Management System

The Problem: Euphoric Clay, a pottery business, was relying on manual spreadsheets to track inventory and sales. This led to data errors, overselling out-of-stock items, and a lack of insight into business performance.

The Solution: I designed and built a live, full-stack web application to solve these issues. I moved the business from spreadsheets to a relational database (3rd Normal Form) to ensure data integrity. The system allows staff to manage products and customers (CRUD) and automatically generates "Low Inventory" and "Top Sales" reports for decision-making.
